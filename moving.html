What to expect on the day of your move

1. To receive a 30 minute call ahead of arrival time. The crew member that you speak to will most likely be the crew leader and will be your go to guy for any of your questions or concerns.

2. Upon arrival you will be greeted by the whole crew (usually consists of 2-4 members) and will be asked for a walk through. During the walk through they may ask questions to get a better feel for items/ etc. Now is the time to make known any concerns you may have for the day ahead.

3. After the walk through is done the crew will begin to load your furniture until completion. Upon completion you will be prompted to do a final walk through to make sure all items that are being moved have been taken and packed.

4. After the final walk through the downhill trek to your new residence begins. Upon arrival you will be prompted for a walk through of the new residence in order to get a feel for where your furniture is going. From there the crew will begin to unload your furniture into your new home.

5. As the crew unload your furniture they will place your furniture where you request and rebuild anything that was taken apart for transit.
